What we're about

Here at BBQ Lovin' we will teach you all you need to know to get started into smoking the best ribs, brisket and everything else to impress your friends and family! Become king of the BBQ with our easy to follow, extreamly tasty recipes which will have you wanting to cook it again and again!

Let's get started

If you are new to BBQ and grilling, start here. These articles and basic recipes give you a solid foundation on BBQ and grilling equipment, ingredients, and techniques. First things first, make sure you have a suitable BBQ for the job at hand. You can smoke on any BBQ, but some are better designed for smoking. Our smoker of choice here at BBQ Lovin' is the Weber smokey mountain!

Must Haves!

Before you even get started, there are some fundamentals that you must have before you can dive into the world of BBQ.

  • A suitable BBQ, like shown above
  • Charcoal
  • Charcoal Chimney
  • Natural Firelighters
  • Thermometer
  • Heat Proof Gloves
  • And ofcourse, Meat!
  • Some cool beers for keeping you hydrated
