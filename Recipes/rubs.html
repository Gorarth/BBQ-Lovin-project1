Our Rubs

Here you will find all the rubs you need to execute our recipes.
Mix all the herbs and spices together to create a beautiful rub for the meat you need!
These can be made and stored for months at a time, so don't worry about making too much!

Memphis Style Pork Rub

Here's our recipe for a great all purpose pork rub. It is carefully formulated to flavor, color, and form the proper crust when cooked at low temps. Can use on anything pork related, however can also work on chicken and vegetables aswell!

  • 3/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup paprika
  • 1/4 cup garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons ground ginger
  • 2 tablespoons onion powder
  • 2 tablespoons rosemary powder

Lemon and Herb Rub

This lemon and herb rub is perfect for chicken. We use it for chicken wings and whole chickens, pulled chicken and everything in between!
When smoking chicken its worth noting that it can sometimes leave a pink colour in the meat, this is perfectly fine aslong as you following the recipe and check the temperature of the meat when ready.

  • 1 cup mixed herbs
  • 1/2 cup garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup table salt
  • 1/2 cup lemon zest powder

Big Bad Beefy Rub

This recipe is perfect for anything beefy, Brisket, Ribs, Steaks and more!
It's best when using this rub to apply to your meat a good 1 hour or more before you plan on cooking. This will cause the rub to stick to your meat better and give it a better crust and flavour!

  • 1 cup coarse sea salt
  • 1 cup cracked black pepper
  • 1/2 cup garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup mixed herbs
  • 1/2 cup onion powder

Larry's Lamb Rub

This lamb rub is full of flavour from herbs. It's amazing on lamb and its also great rubbed chicken or used to season vegetables!

  • 1/2 cup dried rosemary
  • 1/2 cup dried thyme
  • 1/2 cup garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup table salt
  • 1/2 cup ground black pepper
